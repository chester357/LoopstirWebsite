About!

Who Is Loopstir

     At Loopstir, we love to explore and find adventures in our local area, but we don’t always have time and energy to keep up with the best local events which are usually under promoted. This is why we decided to build a mobile app that allows locals to effortlessly discover and promote local events.

Discover Events

     The Loopstir mobile app will keep you in the loop of the best local events by providing a visual appealing feed of events based upon your location, personal interests, and friends. Download Loopstir on the Apple App Store and you’re only one click away from diving into any local community.

Promote Events

     Hosting a cool event? Promote your event directly through the mobile app and share it with everyone in your community. The promotion process quick, affordable, and integrates with the top social media platforms.
