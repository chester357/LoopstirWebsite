About Us

With the Loopstir mobile app you are only one click away from diving into any local community.

Whether you're a local or just tying find what the locals are doing Loopstir will keep you in the loop of new events ranging from nightlife, food & drink, to concerts. Our app will be your personal tour guide for wherever you’re located by recommending events near you and connecting you to people with similar interest as yourself.

Know of a cool event? Help build the social loop by easily sharing an event that you're hosting or an event that you heard about.
